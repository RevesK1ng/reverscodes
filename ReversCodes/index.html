What Is RIVALS?

RIVALS is a fast-paced first-person shooter on Roblox, developed by Nosniy Games. Released on June 28, 2024, it supports cross-platform play (PC, mobile, Xbox, PS5) and centers on tactical 1v1 to 5v5 duels.
