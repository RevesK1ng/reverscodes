What Is RIVALS?

RIVALS is a fast-paced first-person shooter on Roblox, developed by Nosniy Games (led by Nosniy and SenseiWarrior). Released on June 28, 2024, it supports cross-platform play (PC, mobile, Xbox, PS5) and centers on tactical 1v1 to 5v5 duels. Matches are won by being the first to five eliminations, with keys earned in-game used to unlock new weapons, skins, and cosmetic finishers.

Core Gameplay Loop

  • Duel Pads & Matchmaking: Step on duel pads in the lobby for 1v1‚Äì5v5 rounds. Press the green play button to join Team Deathmatch or Free-For-All with global matchmaking.
  • Loadouts & Progression: Each player equips a primary, secondary, melee, and utility weapon. Completing contracts and leaderboards grants additional keys and cosmetics.
  • Maps & Movement: Matches rotate across competitive maps like Arena, Crossroads, Backrooms, and Splash. Advanced movement (sliding, high jumps via explosives) rewards skillful play.
  • Currencies: Keys unlock weapons, skins, and finishers. Win streaks & leaderboards track your ranking on global boards.
