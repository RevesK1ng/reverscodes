🎮 GTA 6 — *Grand Theft Auto VI*

Everything you need to know about the most anticipated game

Official Release Date: May 26, 2026

Set in modern-day Vice City, the game features dual protagonists, Lucia and Jason, with dynamic storylines and next-gen graphics optimized for PS5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC.

Key Features:

  • Dual protagonist system with interwoven narratives
  • Enhanced NPC behavior and advanced AI for a living world
  • Expanded multiplayer and seamless online integration
  • Next-gen graphics pushing console and PC limits

Pricing & Editions:

  • Standard Edition expected to launch at $70
  • Deluxe and Premium editions could reach $100
  • Trailer 2 showcases Vice City nightlife, heists, and character backstories

Delay Rumors: Some insiders suggest a possible push to Fall 2026, but Rockstar and Take-Two's official stance remains May 2026.

⚡ Fortnite Chapter 5 — *OG Season 5*

A blast from the past with classic locations and loot

Season Dates: August 7 – October 3, 2025

The OG Season is back, bringing classic map locations like Paradise Palms, Lazy Links, and the Viking Village, along with a new desert biome and the return of ATK vehicles.

Map Changes & Loot Pool:

  • Returning POIs: Paradise Palms, Lazy Links, Viking Village
  • New Biome: A sprawling new desert area
  • Classic Weapons: Compact SMG, Heavy Sniper, Double-Barrel Shotgun
  • Utility Items: Grappler, Shockwave Grenade, Port-A-Fortress

OG Pass & Game Modes:

  • OG Pass: 45 tiers of nostalgic cosmetics including Redline Racer and Riftreaver Drift skins
  • Expert Mode: A no-SBMM playlist for pure, chaotic fun
  • Future Collabs: Power Rangers, Halo Spartans, and bug-themed skins are coming in Chapter 6.

New biomes, mobs, and experimental features are here

Explore the eerie Pale Garden biome, with its pale oak trees and the hostile "Creaking" mob. New blocks include Hanging Pale Moss and Pale Moss carpets.

🌟 Among Us 2 — *Sequel Status & Features*

Find out what happened to the sequel and what's new

Status: Officially Canceled

In 2020, Innersloth announced that Among Us 2 was canceled. Instead, all planned features are being integrated into the original game to build upon its massive success.

Features Added to Among Us 1:

  • 12-15 player lobbies for more complex games
  • New roles beyond Crewmate/Impostor (e.g., Scientist, Engineer)
  • New game modes like Hide & Seek
  • A robust friends system and improved moderation tools
