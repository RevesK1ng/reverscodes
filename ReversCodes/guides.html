⚔️ How to Get Better at FPS Games

Advanced Techniques:

  • Practice aim training for 30 minutes daily
  • Learn spray patterns for your main weapons
  • Use pre-aiming to reduce reaction time
  • Study professional player demos
  • Focus on crosshair placement at head level
  • Master movement mechanics and strafing
  • Learn common angles and pre-fire spots
  • Practice flick shots and tracking
  • Use sound cues to your advantage
  • Develop game sense through experience

Recommended Training Routines:

  • 10 minutes of aim training (Aim Lab, KovaaK's)
  • 15 minutes of deathmatch practice
  • 5 minutes of spray control practice
  • Review your gameplay recordings

🏆 Battle Royale Survival Tips

Pro Strategies:

  • Always have an escape route planned
  • Use sound cues to track enemy positions
  • Manage inventory efficiently
  • Stay on the edge of the circle early
  • Use vehicles strategically for rotations
  • Learn the map and popular drop locations
  • Master building and editing (for Fortnite)
  • Use cover effectively in gunfights
  • Communicate with your team
  • Stay calm under pressure

Drop Location Strategy:

  • Hot drops for aggressive players
  • Safe drops for passive players
  • Edge drops for strategic positioning
  • Vehicle spawn locations for mobility

💰 Gaming Setup on a Budget

Budget Recommendations:

  • Gaming Mouse: Logitech G203 ($25-30)
  • Keyboard: Redragon K552 ($40-50)
  • Monitor: AOC 24G2 ($180-200)
  • Headset: HyperX Cloud Stinger ($40-50)
  • Mousepad: SteelSeries QcK ($10-15)
  • Webcam: Logitech C270 ($25-30)
  • Microphone: Blue Snowball ($50-60)
  • Desk: IKEA LINNMON ($30-40)
  • Chair: Gaming chair alternatives ($100-150)
  • Lighting: LED strip lights ($15-25)

Setup Tips:

  • Prioritize monitor and mouse for gaming
  • Invest in a good chair for long sessions
  • Proper cable management for clean setup
  • Good lighting reduces eye strain
  • Ergonomic positioning prevents injuries

🎮 Streaming Setup Guide

Essential Equipment:

  • Microphone: Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica AT2020
  • Webcam: Logitech C920 or Razer Kiyo
  • Lighting: Ring light or softbox setup
  • Capture Card: Elgato HD60S for console streaming
  • Green Screen: For professional background removal
  • Stream Deck: For scene transitions and alerts
  • Pop Filter: Reduces unwanted audio noise
  • Shock Mount: Prevents desk vibrations
  • Audio Interface: For professional sound quality
  • Dual Monitors: For chat and stream management

Software Setup:

  • OBS Studio for streaming software
  • Streamlabs for overlays and alerts
  • Voicemeeter for audio mixing
  • Social media for promotion
